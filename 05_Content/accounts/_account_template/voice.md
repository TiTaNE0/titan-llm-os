# <handle> — Canonical Voice

This is the canonical voice for all of this account's channels. Edited by the account owner; AI never modifies it.

Drafting macros load this file and apply [[_shared/voice_pass]] on top of it. See [[00_AGENT_GUIDE]] § 6 for the application contract.

Recommended structure (see [[00_Content_Templates/VOICE_Template]] for the full schema):

- `<voice_fingerprint>` — rhythm, register, ESL texture, sentence shape
- `<writing_laws>` — what to do
- `<avoid>` — what not to do (phrase bank)
- `<decision_rules>` — hallway test, two-beat rule, etc.
- `<final_instruction>` — silent application; never narrate the rules
